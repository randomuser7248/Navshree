Tree Plantation

  Dnyangangotri is a free residential school for mentally challenged children of the age group 6 years to 18 years. It is located near Dhyari village in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Currently they are taking care of 40 children in school. Rudragarajana feels proud to be a one of the helping hand for this organisation. Rudragarjana made donation for Dnyangangotri school to help them for providing free education to Mentally Retarted children.
