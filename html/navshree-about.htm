  • 'Navshree',an organisation related to the traditional Dhol Tasha troupe was established in 2017 with the intention by Mr. Mahesh (Founder), to bring people from all sections of the society together for our beloved ' BAPPA'.

    'Navshree', was formed by few experienced shoulders with 3 Dhols, 1 Tashas and 1 Dhwajs.The count today has reached 35+ Dhols,10+ Tashas and 6+ Dhwajs.'Navshree',at the very beginning had 10 members aged from 10 to 65.The count today has reached a whooping 40 members and still counting. The troupe has seen a rise in the number of women participation in the past years.
